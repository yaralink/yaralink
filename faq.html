FAQ

How to get a website?

Select the type of website that aligns with your requirements, whether it's a personal blog or an online store. Receive an immediate response to your request and engage in a discussion about your project details with a Yaralink manager. Make your payment in Nepali rupees through bank transfer, eSewa, or Khalty. Finally, receive your completed website within the agreed-upon timeframe.

Where i can get Domain name?

A domain name is a distinct name for your website. We suggest registering one with highly recommended sources like Godaddy.com or Dreamhost.com. Opting for a short domain name, such as Example.com, is generally advantageous.

Do you provide marketing service?

No, our expertise lies in creating high-quality and fast-loading websites, which is our primary focus and area of expertise. However, if you require assistance with promoting your project, we can recommend a Marketing Specialist in Nepal who can help you kickstart your marketing efforts.

How much i should pay for website?

By choosing Yaralink, you can eliminate recurring expenses such as monthly hosting fees or SSL certificate costs for your website. Take a look at our price table for more information. With Yaralink, you only need to make an annual payment for your own domain (typically around $5-12/year). Additionally, there is a one-time payment to set up your site with Yaralink, as outlined in our price table. For a Blog, Company Site, or Chat Bot, there are no further payments for life. However, if you opt for an Online Store, please note that Shopify requires a monthly payment for using their software, starting from $5 per month.

Why your prices is less than others?

At Yaralink, we leverage cutting-edge technology tools to create websites, enabling us to eliminate unnecessary expenses. Additionally, we save costs on office rent by meeting our clients in public places as friends to discuss project details. However, the most significant reason to choose us is our extensive experience and expertise in the field.
